**Effective date:** March 14, 2026

Stillpoint is an ambient sound mixer that runs entirely on your device. This policy explains what data Stillpoint collects, stores, and transmits.

## What Stillpoint does NOT collect

- No personal information
- No account or login
- No analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports
- No usage tracking
- No cookies

## Local-only operation

Stillpoint runs a local audio server on your machine. All audio processing, mixer state, and preferences stay on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

## Custom sounds

When you add custom WAV files, they are read from your local filesystem only. File names and paths are never transmitted off-device.

## Network access

Stillpoint does not make any outbound network requests. The application communicates only between its own local components (UI on `localhost:5177` and server on `localhost:3456`).

## Third-party services

Stillpoint does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

## Children's privacy

Stillpoint does not collect any data from any user, including children.

## Changes to this policy

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at [github.com/mcp-tool-shop-org/stillpoint](https://github.com/mcp-tool-shop-org/stillpoint/issues).
